Our Story

Built on
Generations of Craft

"We don't just make furniture — we build heirlooms that outlast trends."

Pankaj Furniture was born in the heart of Jamshedpur from a simple belief: every family deserves furniture that is both beautiful and built to last. For over 15 years, our master craftsmen have handcrafted pieces for thousands of homes across Jharkhand.

We use premium-grade timber, quality hardware, and traditional joinery techniques combined with modern design sensibilities. When you order from us, you're commissioning a piece made specifically for your space.
